Dubhe's Story & Mission

Dubhe started its work end of 2020 within a hackathon and several more events followed, such as the ImpactChallenge. And an "Impact Challenge" is what drives our daily work, namely the challenge of contributing to a better world by reducing air pollution in cities and therefore improving quality of life for its citizens. Together with corporates and the government we are aiming at making cities more livable. The availability of immense amounts of unused data is making this possible.

The name "Dubhe": Dubhe is the biggest star in the Ursa Major (a bear) constellation. The idea was born with the goal to tackle the challenge of air pollution by using satellite data, hence data from space. This leads to the motto - Beating Air Pollution From Space.

The Team

As Dubhe's members were born and raised in several continents, and small to big cities, they have themselves experienced the problem of air pollution. The team consisting of engineers from a diverse technical, business and environmental background brings the necessary knowledge to tackle the challenge and lend their hand towards a more sustainable future.
